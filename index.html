Who We Are

A Community
Built on Unity

NYEKUSA — Nyeri County University Students Association — is a vibrant student body at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT), founded in 2019 to bring together students who come from Nyeri County or simply admire the county's rich culture and heritage.

Membership is open to all. We welcome individuals from every background who share our values of unity, friendship, and active community engagement. Together we foster strong bonds through charity events, mentorship programs, scenic hikes, community outreach, and unforgettable social gatherings.
