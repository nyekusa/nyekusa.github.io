WHO ARE WE?

NYEKUSA is a student association at Dedan Kimathi University of Technology (DeKUT), founded in 2019. The organization was established to bring together students who either come from Nyeri County or simply admire and appreciate the county.

Membership is not limited to students from Nyeri County. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds who share our values of unity, friendship, and community engagement.

Like many vibrant student organizations, we foster strong bonds among our members through various activities including charity events, mentorship programs, hikes, community outreach initiatives, team-building activities, and other social engagements.
